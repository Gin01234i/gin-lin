About Gin Lin

Gin Lin is a researcher and curator specializing in contemporary art, with a focus on Asian and Asian diasporic practices. Grounded in postcolonial theory, diaspora studies, and feminist critique, her work examines how identity, memory, and place are interwoven and negotiated through contemporary art.

With experience across private museums, galleries, and auction houses in Shanghai, Taipei, and New York, Lin brings a distinctly cross-cultural perspective to her practice. She has curated and coordinated exhibitions across independent, institutional, and commercial contexts in the United States and Asia. Her research-driven approach emphasizes close collaboration with artists and the translation of critical frameworks into public-facing exhibitions, programs, and events.

Drawing on her background in finance alongside an M.A. in Contemporary Art from Sotheby's Institute of Art, Lin integrates scholarly research with strategic and financial insight. Her practice balances curatorial vision with sustainable production, audience development, and commercial collaboration, bridging academic research with creative, commercially informed communication.

Gin Lin 是一位專注於當代藝術的研究者與策展人，具備橫跨台北、上海與紐約的美術館、基金會、畫廊與拍賣行經驗。結合金融背景與跨文化經驗，她發展出兼具策略深度與研究力度的策展與收藏實踐。她曾任紐約佳士得戰後與當代藝術部研究員，而作為獨立策展人，她以跨文化視角為核心，致力於放大被忽略的聲音，並搭建多元開放的對話平台。
